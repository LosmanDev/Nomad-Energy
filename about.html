Why Solar?
Puntland has one of the world’s greatest solar resources, but also some of the highest electricity prices. Solar energy technology is now a reliable and affordable option for providing power and hot water.
Businesses investing in solar energy can expect the following:
  • Reduction in electricity and diesel expenses
  • High quality power with very little maintenance required
  • Ability to add capacity incrementally over time
  • Reduce noise, emissions & maintenance of diesel gensets
